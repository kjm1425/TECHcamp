About

Our TECH Camps help get more girls inspired and excited about computing through fun and enganging activities. Girls create their own Android apps, program Sphero robots, and more.

Join us for TECH Camp every Spring. Two Saturday events and one overnight. For just $50 for the entire experience, you won't want to miss this.

Our Mission

It is a well know fact that there is a lack of women in computing. Computing, however is the future; there will be 1.4 million job openings in computer-related fields by 2020. Our mission is to get more girls interested in computing at a younger age. Targeting middle school girls, we hope to inspire them and ignite their passion for technical pursuits at a young age. We hope that after attending a TECH Camps program, participants take home new technical skills, a greater awareness of what computing can do, and hopefully a budding passion for computing.
